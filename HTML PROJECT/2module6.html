Quarter 2- Module 6: Determining the Dependent and Independent Variables and Finding the Domain and Range of a Function

This module focuses on determining the dependent and independent variables of a function and finding the domain and range. Students will learn how to analyze the behavior of functions and identify the variables that influence their output.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Defining dependent and independent variables
  • Understanding the domain and range of a function
  • Identifying restrictions on domain and range
  • Graphing functions and analyzing their behavior

By mastering the concepts presented in this module, students will be able to analyze functions more deeply, gaining insights into their behavior and making connections between algebraic representations and graphical interpretations.
