Mathematics: Quarter 1 - Module 1A Factoring Polynomials

This module is designed and written to help you factor polynomials completely using different techniques. In all lessons, you are given the opportunity to use your prior knowledge and skills in multiplying and dividing polynomials. Activities are also given to process your knowledge and skills acquired, deepen and transfer your understanding of the different lessons. The scope of this module enables you to use it in many different learning situations. The lessons are arranged to follow the standard sequence of the course.
