Quarter 2- Module 1: Differentiating Linear Inequalities and Linear Equations in Two Variables

This module covers the differentiation between linear inequalities and linear equations in two variables. Students will learn to identify the characteristics and properties of linear inequalities and equations and understand how to represent them graphically.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Distinguishing between linear inequalities and linear equations
  • Understanding the graphical representation of linear inequalities and equations
  • Solving linear inequalities and equations algebraically
  • Applying the solutions to real-world problems

By mastering the concepts presented in this module, students will develop a solid foundation in understanding and working with linear inequalities and equations, which are fundamental concepts in algebra and mathematical modeling.
