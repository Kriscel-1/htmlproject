Quarter 1- Module 4: Simplifying Rational Algebraic Expression

This module continues the study of rational algebraic expressions, focusing on simplifying them to their most reduced form. Simplifying rational expressions is crucial in various mathematical contexts, including solving equations and graphing functions.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Review of factoring techniques
  • Identifying common factors in rational expressions
  • Using the least common denominator (LCD) to simplify
  • Clearing fractions in equations
  • Applying simplification techniques to real-world problems

By mastering the techniques presented in this module, students will be able to simplify complex rational expressions efficiently and apply them to solve various mathematical problems.
