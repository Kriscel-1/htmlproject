Quarter 2- Module 14: Writing Proofs (Direct and Indirect)

This module focuses on writing proofs using direct and indirect methods. Students will learn the principles of mathematical proof and how to apply them to solve problems and establish mathematical truths.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Understanding the structure of mathematical proofs
  • Using direct proof techniques to establish mathematical statements
  • Applying indirect proof techniques such as proof by contradiction and contrapositive
  • Recognizing and avoiding common pitfalls in proof writing

By mastering the concepts presented in this module, students will develop skills in logical reasoning and problem-solving, which are essential for success in higher-level mathematics and related disciplines.
