Quarter 2- Module 9: Determining the Relationship Between the Hypothesis and the Conclusion of an If-then Statement

This module focuses on understanding the relationship between the hypothesis and the conclusion of an if-then statement. Students will learn about conditional statements and how to analyze their logical structure.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Identifying the hypothesis and conclusion of an if-then statement
  • Recognizing the different types of conditional statements
  • Understanding the truth value of conditional statements
  • Applying logical reasoning to analyze if-then statements

By mastering the concepts presented in this module, students will develop critical thinking skills and be able to analyze and evaluate logical statements, which are essential for problem-solving and decision-making in various disciplines.
