Quarter 2- Module 4: Solving Systems of Linear Inequalities in Two Variables

This module focuses on solving systems of linear inequalities in two variables. Students will learn techniques to find the solution sets and interpret the feasible regions represented by the intersection of multiple inequalities.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Understanding the concept of a system of linear inequalities
  • Finding the solution set of a system graphically and algebraically
  • Interpreting the feasible region of a system in the context of real-world applications
  • Optimizing solutions to systems of inequalities

By mastering the concepts presented in this module, students will be able to analyze and solve complex problems involving multiple linear inequalities, gaining valuable skills applicable to various fields such as economics, engineering, and environmental science.
