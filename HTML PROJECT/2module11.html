Quarter 2- Module 11: Determining the Inverse, Converse and Contrapositive of an If-then Statement

This module focuses on determining the inverse, converse, and contrapositive of an if-then statement. Students will learn how to analyze and manipulate logical statements to identify their different forms.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Defining the inverse, converse, and contrapositive of an if-then statement
  • Recognizing the relationship between different forms of logical statements
  • Applying logical equivalences to derive equivalent statements
  • Analyzing the truth value of different forms of statements

By mastering the concepts presented in this module, students will develop skills in logical reasoning and be able to analyze and manipulate statements effectively, which are essential for problem-solving and decision-making in various disciplines.
