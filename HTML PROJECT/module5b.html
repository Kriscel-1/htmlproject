Quarter 1- Module 5b: Adding and Subtracting Similar and Dissimilar Rational Algebraic Expression

This module covers the addition and subtraction of rational algebraic expressions, both similar and dissimilar. Understanding these operations is crucial in simplifying expressions and solving equations involving rational expressions.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Adding and subtracting rational expressions with like denominators
  • Adding and subtracting rational expressions with unlike denominators
  • Simplifying the result of addition and subtraction
  • Identifying restrictions on variables
  • Applications of adding and subtracting rational expressions

By mastering the concepts presented in this module, students will be able to confidently perform addition and subtraction operations on rational expressions and apply them to solve various mathematical problems.
