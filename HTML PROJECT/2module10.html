Quarter 2- Module 10: Transforming a Statement into an Equivalent If-then Statement

This module focuses on transforming a statement into an equivalent if-then statement. Students will learn techniques to rewrite statements in if-then form and analyze their logical equivalence.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Understanding the logical structure of if-then statements
  • Identifying equivalent statements
  • Applying logical equivalences to rewrite statements
  • Analyzing the truth value of equivalent statements

By mastering the concepts presented in this module, students will develop skills in logical reasoning and be able to transform and analyze statements effectively, which are essential for problem-solving and decision-making in various contexts.
