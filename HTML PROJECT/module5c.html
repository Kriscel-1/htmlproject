Quarter 1- Module 5c: Solving Problems Involving Rational Algebraic Expression

This module focuses on solving problems that involve rational algebraic expressions. These problems may include equations, inequalities, and word problems that require the manipulation and solution of rational expressions.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Solving equations with rational expressions
  • Solving inequalities with rational expressions
  • Applying algebraic techniques to word problems
  • Identifying extraneous solutions
  • Applications of solving problems involving rational expressions

By mastering the concepts presented in this module, students will be able to confidently solve a variety of mathematical problems involving rational algebraic expressions and apply their problem-solving skills to real-world scenarios.
