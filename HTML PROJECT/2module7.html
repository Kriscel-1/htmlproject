Quarter 2- Module 7: Illustrating and Graphing a Linear Function

This module focuses on illustrating and graphing linear functions. Students will learn how to graph linear equations, analyze their characteristics, and interpret their graphs in real-world contexts.

Key topics covered in this module include:

  • Graphing linear equations using slope-intercept form
  • Identifying slope and y-intercept from the equation of a line
  • Interpreting slope as a rate of change
  • Applying linear functions to model real-world scenarios

By mastering the concepts presented in this module, students will be able to graph linear functions accurately, analyze their behavior, and apply them to solve problems in various fields such as economics, physics, and engineering.
